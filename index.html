About Me

My name is Paul Pham, and I proudly serve as a Sergeant in the Minnesota Army National Guard. I’m currently pursuing a degree in Criminology at the University of Minnesota Duluth, with the goal of becoming a Police Officer and continuing my commitment to public service.

My military experience has shaped me into a disciplined and resilient leader, instilling values of integrity, teamwork, and accountability — qualities I carry with me in both my academic and personal life. Outside of uniform and the classroom, I’m passionate about fitness, gaming, and cooking.
