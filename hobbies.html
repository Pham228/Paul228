Athletic Achievements

I started this fitness journey at 166 lbs with basically no muscle. I cut weight, shipped to Basic Combat Training (BCT), and when I returned I bulked and committed to training consistently since August 2018. At BCT I finished top of my company on the Army Physical Fitness Test (APFT). Since then I’ve kept the momentum—building strength, discipline, and consistency. I’m now 146 lbs, training 6 days a week, and proud of how far I’ve come.
